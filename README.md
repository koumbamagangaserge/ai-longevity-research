# AI & Longevity Research

This repository explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and human longevity, with a focus on advancing research and innovation aimed at extending healthy human life.

## Research Focus

- Organ regeneration and tissue engineering
- Cellular aging and rejuvenation
- Neurodegeneration and neuron regeneration
- AI-driven biomedical analysis

## Strategic Vision

My objective is to contribute to the development of AI-driven solutions that address some of the most critical challenges facing humanity today, particularly those related to aging, disease, and longevity.

This work reflects a broader commitment to leveraging artificial intelligence not only for business performance, but also for advancing human health and well-being.

## Approach

This repository is intended as a research-oriented space, bringing together:

- Conceptual frameworks
- Applied AI perspectives
- Emerging trends in longevity science

## Long-Term Objective

To actively participate in the global effort to extend human lifespan and improve quality of life through the integration of artificial intelligence and biomedical innovation.
